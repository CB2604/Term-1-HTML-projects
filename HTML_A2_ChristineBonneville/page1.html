DENTAL OFFICE STAFF

  • Chairside Team
    1. Dentist
      This is the licensed doctor who performs the dental surgeries and restorations on the patients. They also do dental check-ups.
    2. Dental Assistant
      This is the trained professional who assists the dentist during procedures, takes care of preparing the room and sterilizing the equipment, and prepares the patient. They can also teach preventative care depending on their ceritification level.
    3. Hygienist
      This is the licensed professional who does cleanings and check-ups on the patients. They can also perform some restorative work.
  • Office Team
    1. Treatment Coordinator
      This is the trained professional who prepares treatment plans and explains them to the patients.
    2. Receptionist
      This is the trained professional who schedules appointments, recieves payments and takes care of sending insurance claims for the patients.
    3. Administration Manager
      This is a trained professional who manages the office, takes care of its accounts, and covers many HR duties and scheduling.
